About Me

I am a fourth-year undergraduate and Early Admit M.Eng. student at in Mechanical Engineering at Cornell University focused on designing and analyzing performance-driven physical systems.

As a coxswain for Cornell’s Lightweight Rowing team and an avid cyclist, my perspective in athletics has shaped how I think about performance, where small design decisions can have measurable impacts on system behavior.

A question that drives my work is: how can I design and build systems that perform reliably and efficiently in demanding environments?

Manufacturing
