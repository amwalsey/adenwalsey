About Me

Hi, I’m Aden Walsey, a Mechanical Engineering student at Cornell University and an Early Admit M.Eng. student focused on design, manufacturing, and performance-driven systems.

I’m especially interested in building physical products that perform reliably under real-world constraints, whether that means improving manufacturability, validating designs through simulation, or refining systems for better performance.

Outside the classroom, I’m usually on the bike, in the boat, or making something in the shop.
